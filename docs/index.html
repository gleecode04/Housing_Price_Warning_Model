Early-Warning Model for U.S. Housing Price Surges

Predicting Future Housing Market Opportunities Using Machine Learning

Machine Learning Real Estate Analytics Time Series Prediction

1. Introduction / Background

Housing prices often jump after local shocks (jobs, migration, supply constraints). We aim to predict, ahead of time, which ZIPs/counties will experience future surges in home values. Hedonic and spatial-econometric literature shows prices depend on amenities and neighboring markets [1], [2]; supply elasticity and constraints amplify cycles [3]. Modern ensembles capture non-linear, interacting drivers [4], [5].

2. Problem Definition

Problem Statement

Given data up to time t, predict whether area i will be in the top decile/quintile of ZHVI YoY growth over t+6‚Ä¶t+12 months (classification), and/or forecast future YoY %Œî (regression).

Motivation

Identify investable areas before broad appreciation (e.g., pre-2020 Miami-like runs). Use probability-ranked screens for capital allocation and due diligence.
